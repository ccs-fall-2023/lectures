# Resources

## Naming Conventions

* `snake_case`
   Separated by underscore
* `kebab-case`
   Separated by hyphen
* `camelCase`
   First letter of every subsequent word is capitalized
* `PascalCase`
   First character of every word is capitalized
* `UPPERCASE`
   All characters uppercase
